Ticket management

A central hub to identify, delegate, resolve, & record borrower issues.

Untimely addressing of borrower issues is one of the top reasons that cause customers to leave their lender.

Research & Opportunity

We went to new hire trainings for customer support staff and followed them around for a couple of weeks to understand the complexities of resolving customer issues.

We started noticing a few problem areas:

 Delegating work
While agents were efficient at solving simple issues, complex issues that required help from back of house teams like underwriters and processors would often experience long delays or were ignored altogether.

 Issue escalation
Other than sympathising with the user's discontent, support agents often had few options to escalate issues that were blocked by other functions.

 Complex issues
Our previous ticketing solution was built to help support agents focus on one issue at a time. The problem was that these conversation would often evolve and branch into multiple items and follow up issues. These would require ad hoc tracking and were sometimes lost.

 Communicating challenges
As customer issues were passed from one agent to another, context from previous communications with customers were frequently lost, which often led to redundant conversations and unhappy borrowers.
If we can break consumer issues down to small, structured tasks, we can understand and continuously improve the efficiency and quality of issue resolution.

Defining our approach

Figuring out the pieces

We started exploring designs and testing them with users to make sure our solutions would fit both company needs and user habits.

After reaching general alignment on design directions, we began thinking about how the product can be broken up to phases and made sure we addressed the different flows and states.

Results


Tickets Dashboard

The tickets page start agents with a view of the unclaimed tickets queue so they can understand, claim, and address them. The agent can use search, filters & sort to look for anything more specific.


Details Page

In detail view, users can work with teammates and move the ticket along by understanding the issue history, modifying watchers and assignees, as well as closing the ticket.


Delegating tasks

Complicated issues that need support from a specialized functions can be escalated as ticket tasks.

Ticket tasks are assigned to a specific team or person, notifying them upon assignment, and include all of the context from from the ticket.


Passing Notes

Ticket notes let agents attach additional context and tag other users to ensure that all information needed for resolution is centralized. Notes can be added and viewed in Tickets as well as the Tasks page so all staff involved can communicate without navigating to an unfamiliar product.

We staffed a pilot team of 8 to prove out the product and saw promosing results. The team achived 0 unassigned ticket in queue >80% of the days.

As the product rolled out, we were able to reduce our SLA time from 48+ to < 24 hours.
