Discover & Explore

Nudging job seekers to discover & explore the jobs they want but don't know about yet.

More than 50% of the daily searches on Zip are ran without a keyword.

Research & Opportunity

We previously treated empty searches as mistakes, and sent users to a generalized experience where they saw a jobs from a manually curated list of industries. This experience had poor conversion, and we hypothesized that the volumn is too big to just be a mistake. So we recruited real job seekers to learn more.

We talked to a bunch of recent job seekers and noticed some common threads:

  Career Fluid- Many entry level workers are not fixated on a specific career yet. A restaurant wait staff is sometime just as happy find a retail role next and vice versa.

 Trouble seeing- Many job seekers felt like there must be more job titles they should be exploring but did not know of a easy way to discover them.

 3x drop-off- users who blank search are many times likely to finish their session without applying.

 Feature, not bug- Job boards generally treat empty searches as mistakes, but many job seekers saw them as a tool to explore opportunities.
Job seekers often aren't aware of most of the job titles they should be searching for. Empty searches are often the results of job seekers exploring their options.

Exploring solutions

We ran a bunch of A/B tests over the course of 2 months to see if nudges and suggestions at key points in the experience would increase application & match rate.
