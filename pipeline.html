Loan Pipeline

Advanced filtering tool that helps mortgage professionals stay hyper focused on the work that matters in an ever-changing market.

The type of loans that mortgage workers care about changes dramatically from week to week. With every change, the traits that matter changes, and users need to learn a new way to parse for the files that matter.

Research Insights

We were told it was not uncommon for the teams to ....and wanted to learn more
The PM, EM, and myself shadowed dozens of mortgage professionals in every stage of the loan process to understand their work flow, concerns, interactions, process and pain points.

Here are some patterns we noticed:
 Changing winds- The type of loans that mortgage professionals care about is constantly changing - any change in capital market conditions, company capacity, politics, policy, and an infinite number of other factors could mean what used to be top priority is suddenly the last things that should be worked on. It's not abnormal for these kinds of complete shift in priorities to happen every couple of weeks.

 Detective work- The traits that makes certain loans high priority and not others are often buried in the details of the loan. Without proper indexing of loan traits, teams often had to parse through files one by one to figure out which is worth working on.
If mortgage work can be broken down to small, contained tasks and be shown with context - then expensive work that used to require specialized professionals can be done by a non-specialized person at greater efficiency with less errors.

Exploring solutions

After testing successfully on a general design direction, we started working out the different states & flows to address all of the likely scenarios in our MVP use case.

Results

Pipeline enable mortgage professionals to hyper focus on the work that matters to them by quickly fiters for the loan traits that matter right now.
Between the mostly standardized set of traits in mortgage and the ability for users to add tags to file, Pipeline is generally able to be set to accommodate any kind of workflow.
Pipeline starts with a default set of filter categories and table columns based on what is commonly used by a user's peer at that time. Additional filter categories and columns can be added as needed.
A detail side sheet enables mortgage relationship managers to quickly understand the status of files in their cohort.


Workflow Builder and Tasklist allowed managers to track and continuously optimize their workflow strategies.

Early MVP results showed complex task completion time improve an average of 6.2% (From 57 to 53.5 hours including time in queue).
