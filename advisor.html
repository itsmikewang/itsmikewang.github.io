Career Advisor

Helping job seekers discover all of the opportunities they deserve, and bridge the gap for those they don't qualify for yet.

Job seeking and job working are separate skills.

High quality job seekers don't always appear that way to hiring managers because of poorly constructed resumes and profiles.

Research & Opportunity

We dug through the millions of job applications we've facilitated and found some patterns:

The way hiring managers write job descriptions is generally different from the way candidates applying to those same jobs write about their experience. Perfectly qualified candidates often do not appear to be a good match. Educating both sides means employers discover more high quality candidates and job seekers are able to better present and improve themselves.

Employers and job seekers often describe the same things differently. If we can leverage ML to make suggestions and nudge the two side towards each other, we can activate lots of missed great matches.

Exploring solutions

We started with our data & best hypothesis, worked through rounds of user testing, and slowly shaped the project into something useful.

Results


Career advisor compares profile information to common details in job postings to help high quality candidates can improve how they are presented while less qualified ones can see what they should work on.

Resume keywords, certifications, education levels & ATS friendly resume builder to help job seekers gauge how they stack up against the competition.
